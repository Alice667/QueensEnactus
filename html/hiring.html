Join Us!

TOGETHER we have the power to create a BETTER tomorrow!



Enactus Queen’s needs passionate like-minded individuals to help us grow the club and our projects. We need individuals on the operations side (marketing, frosh reps) and the projects side (creating and managing projects).

How to Apply?

Email your resume and answers to the following application questions to queensenactus@gmail.com. (note: some positions require an additional application question, look at position description for further detail)

  • Why are you interested in joining Enactus Queen’s? (recommend 100-150 words)
  • What do you believe is your most valuable skill when working in a team-oriented environment? (recommended 100-150 words).


Job Positions Avaliable

Position Description
Entrepreneur The role of an entrepreneur is to bring fresh ideas and innovative thinking to the table, with the ultimate goal of driving the club's success and growth. As an entrepreneur, you will be responsible for identifying new opportunities and developing creative solutions to address them. You'll collaborate with fellow club members to generate and pitch ideas for events, programs, and initiatives that align with the club's mission and objectives. In this role, you'll also have the opportunity to build valuable skills in leadership, problem-solving, and strategic thinking, all while making a positive impact on the university community. If you're someone who thrives on creativity, initiative, and an entrepreneurial mindset, we'd love to have you join our team!

Application Question: What is your social-minded/sustainable business idea, or are you interested in any particular area of social impact?

Innovation Director As the Innovation Director of our university club, you will play a critical role in driving our organization's success by fostering a culture of innovation and creativity. In this position, you will be responsible for identifying and implementing new ideas, programs, and initiatives that align with our club's mission and objectives. You will work closely with other members of the executive team to develop strategies for innovation, while also encouraging and empowering all club members to share their own ideas and perspectives. Additionally, you will be responsible for staying up-to-date with the latest trends, technologies, and best practices in your field, and applying this knowledge to help drive the club forward. If you're someone who is passionate about innovation, has a track record of implementing creative solutions, and is excited about the opportunity to make a real impact on our university community, we would love to have you join our team as Innovation Director.

Marketing Director The Marketing Director will be tasked with ensuring all marketing related material, for all teams, is up to date. You will be creating the publications and graphics in written and graphic deliverables for all team requirements.

The successful applicant will need to develop a comprehensive marketing and communications strategy for the upcoming year. Thus, strong written communication and graphic design skills will be considered an asset, as would experience with design programs like Adobe Photoshop. This will be a busy role, and we are looking for an individual who can take the initiative and make strict deadlines. The time commitment will range from 1-4 hours per week and may be higher in periods such as March Hiring, Winter Hiring, and competition times.

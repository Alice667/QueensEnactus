Competition

Take a look at how our impact has performed on the big stage!



Enactus competitions showcase the impacts of students from across Canada and the world. These competitions inspire excellence amongst students through the creation of projects which seek to empower communities and shape the economic, social and environmental landscape of communities on a regional, national and international scale.

Regionals

Second weekend in March

Enactus Canada Regionals is an annual competition that brings together university and college students from across Canada to showcase their entrepreneurial skills and innovative solutions to social, environmental, and economic challenges. During the event, student-led Enactus teams present their projects and initiatives to a panel of judges, who evaluate their impact, sustainability, and entrepreneurial approach.

Nationals

First week of May

Winning teams from each regionals competition advance to the Enactus Canada National Exposition, where they compete against other regional champions for the chance to represent Canada at the Enactus World Cup.
